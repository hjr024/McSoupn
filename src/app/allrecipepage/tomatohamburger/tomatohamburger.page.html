Tomato Hamburger Soup Tomato Hamburger Soup Bookmark Recipe Save Recipe Ingredients: 1 can (46 ounces) V8 juice 2 packages (16 ounces each)
frozen mixed vegetables 1 pound ground beef, cooked and
drained 1 can (10-3/4 ounces) condensed
cream of mushroom soup,
undiluted 2 teaspoons dried minced onion Salt and pepper to taste Directions: In a 5-qt. slow cooker, combine the
first 5 ingredients. Cover and cook
on high for 4-5 hours or until
heated through. Season with salt
and pepper.
